The University Of TorontoInformation, Masters (F17)Other via Other on 3 Mar 2017 O3 Mar 2017
  • Hey guys, about the ROSI thing. They usually send out acceptances in batches, so even if you don't have a letter now or Invited on ROSI, keep checking every week or so. They send out acceptances and see who accepts so they know how many they can accept the next round. Also, sgs usually takes a long time to update (some people's still says under review when they are already enrolled in the program.). Pay more attention to your mailbox and ROSI status. Lastly, they might not reject people yet (unless you don't need minimum requirements). So if you don't hear back right away with a yes/no, you are most likely going to be wait listed until the official deadline closes and they make their decision about the applications that came in later. I know that method sucks but part of me hopes that happens because I have yet to submit, so I hope I have a fair chance of getting in as well if I'm a strong enough candidate. Be patient everyone, if it is meant to be, it will happen!
