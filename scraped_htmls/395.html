  • To the poster below: I didn't apply to UCSD, but the type of email you received is not uncommon. Some programs send out decisions fairly impersonally but many arrange for professors you are interested in to contact you directly and express their excitement. You should be proud of getting in, but be wary of reading too much into the email – this is common and not something that should necessarily sway your decision. In almost all cases, it's best to go with the program ranked the highest (this does make a very real difference for job prospects), unless you're talking about programs that are both in, say, the top 10, or both in the top 20, or both in the top 30. Consider the funding arrangements, too!
