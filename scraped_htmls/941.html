New York University ( NYU GSAS )Data Science, PhD (F17)Other via Other on 14 Apr 2017 O14 Apr 2017
  • To the posters below who are wondering about how to access Albert, you first need to activate your NetID. If you\'re like me, you would\'ve only received a UID (University ID) which starts with N and has a bunch of numbers. To get your NetID, you need to go to "start.nyu.edu", click on the link at the top right corner that reads, \"Don\'t know your NetID\". You will then be prompted to enter your UID and date of birth, upon which you will see your NetID. After that, you need to activate your NetID by following the steps and subsequently log into your \"NYU Home\". Here is where Albert resides. Access him by clicking on the Academics tab, and then logging into your Albert account. You will need to enter your NetID and password to login. Now, if you\'re still like me, your admit type will read \"Entering with an UGRD Degree\", and that\'s how you know you\'re still stuck in the applicant limbo. Let\'s hope we get our catharsis soon!
